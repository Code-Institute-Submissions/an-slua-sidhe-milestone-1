NEW Corr Mhóna album!

(08/05/19) After many years of hibernation, West Cork's finest have returned with a new album: 'Dair'. Containing 45 minutes of dense and complex melodies, Dair is more than worth the wait! We are very proud to announce that we will be releasing this on a special edition digipak CD, with a free poster inside each one, which contains album artwork and a lyrics sheet. This will be available on the shop on this site and on Bandcamp. See the Player (only available on desktop, gábh ár leathscéal) at the top right for a pre-release version of the title track! There is also a Teaser Video in the Media section which has a few snippets from different tracks. Enjoy! Further details of the album launch date, as well as the opening of our online shop (selling Corr Mhóna CDs, merchandise and more) will be available here very soon. The new CD and merch with the art from the CD will be available exclusively from our shop. Beir bua a chairde, agus tóg go bog é!

Online Shop Coming Soon

(22/03/19) Exciting developments here in Corr Mhóna land; we are currently in the process of constructing our own online shop. It will be available on this very website in the near future, and will cut out the middle man. This means that all profits will go straight to the band, but also that we will be able to offer our merch and CDs for less! Copies of our CDs and T-shirts, along with all forthcoming future products, will be just a couple of clicks away. The shop will be accessed via a 'SHOP' tab in the navigation bar at the top of the page (this is currently crossed out, and will be clickable when ready). We will have some more very exciting news for you very soon... Bí ag éisteacht linn!

Corr Mhóna play the Siege of Limerick

(22/11/18) Corr Mhóna are proud to announce that we are once again gracing the stage on Sunday 21st of April at the mighty Siege of Limerick! This twice annual festival in Limerick is the highlight of the calendar year for metal fans in Ireland. Hundreds of fans head down to swarm over the city for the bank holiday weekend. This is our third time attending the event, and each time it has become bigger and more professional. It is incredible to think that there is still no entrance fee, and all these great bands are available for free. We will be playing a couple of tracks from our upcoming album at this, so if you fancy a sneak peek, come on down and get stuck in!
